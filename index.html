Assistant Social Officer – IEDC MEA
During my tenure as Assistant Social Officer, I played a key role in bridging entrepreneurship with social responsibility. I worked on initiatives that encouraged students to design solutions for real-world challenges, collaborated with NGOs and local communities, and promoted sustainable innovation.
I was actively involved in organizing workshops, awareness campaigns, and flagship events such as ENOVA (national-level flagship innovation fest) and INSTINTO’25 (entrepreneurship expo), along with entrepreneurship awareness drives and social outreach programs, ensuring that IEDC’s mission extended beyond the campus. This experience not only helped me contribute to society but also strengthened my leadership, communication, and teamwork skills, while shaping my perspective on the role of entrepreneurship in creating social impact.
